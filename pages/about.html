About

Bodyboarding is so much fun!

I got into bodyboarding around 23 years ago when I came down to Cornwall on a family holiday. I remember seeing people riding along the waves, doing spins, and thinking that I would like a go at that! I would read about bodyboarding in magazines and watch videos to learn what to do! Eventually I started taking part in competitions! This led me to taking part in the World Bodyboard Games in 2011. I still love getting to go bodyboarding whenever I can.

Hopefully this website will help you to learn all about bodyboarding!

Author: Olivia Smedley
